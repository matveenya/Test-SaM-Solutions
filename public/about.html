Company Profile

SaM Solutions is an international software solution development service provider with over twenty-five years of experience in the information technology market. The main directions of the company are the development of custom software in the markets of the USA, Europe and the CIS, as well as consulting services within the framework of development processes. Regional offices of SaM Solutions are located in the USA, Germany, the Netherlands and Eastern European countries - Lithuania, Latvia, Belarus and Ukraine.

SaM Solutions employs highly qualified specialists in various technological fields. The company constantly invests in improving the professional skills of its employees, thereby ensuring the effective implementation of projects under development, using the latest technological solutions.
